OUR REGION AND DESTINATIONS

Colombian Massif

The Colombian Massif (Macizo Colombiano) is a vital ecological region in southern Colombia, where the Andes mountains converge. It is the birthplace of major rivers and home to diverse ecosystems, making it one of the most important centers of biodiversity and water in the country.

San Agustín

San Agustín is located in the department of Huila, nestled within the Colombian Massif (Macizo Colombiano), in southern Colombia, known for its rich archaeological heritage, Andean landscapes, and unique biodiversity. Surrounded by mountains and cloud forests, it’s a paradise for birdwatching and connecting with nature.

Destinations

Discover a variety of birdwatching routes in the heart of San Agustín, where lush landscapes and ancient history come together. Our tours are designed for all levels—from beginners to experienced birders—and take you through cloud forests, riverbanks, coffee plantations, and archaeological parks.
